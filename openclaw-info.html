OpenClaw Resources - Official Information & Documentation

OpenClaw is a self-hosted, open-source AI assistant that connects your messaging platforms to AI agents. Formerly known as Moltbot and Clawdbot, OpenClaw enables you to run your own personal AI assistant on your own hardware.

Key Features

Multi-Platform Messaging Integration

OpenClaw connects to various messaging platforms including WhatsApp, Telegram, Discord, Slack, Signal, and iMessage, allowing you to interact with your AI assistant wherever you are.

Self-Hosted Privacy

Run your AI assistant on your own hardware, ensuring that your conversations and data remain private and under your control. No third-party servers logging your interactions.

Extensible with Skills

OpenClaw uses AgentSkills-compatible skill folders to extend functionality. There are 50+ integrations with apps and services you already use, and the community continuously develops new skills.

AI Model Agnostic

Works with various AI providers including Claude, GPT, DeepSeek, and others. Connect your preferred large language model to your OpenClaw instance.

Popular OpenClaw Skills

OpenClaw's functionality can be extended through skills. Here are some popular ones:

  • gitlab-manager - Manage GitLab repositories, merge requests, and issues via API
  • gitload - Download files or folders from GitHub without cloning the entire repository
  • domaindetails - Look up domain WHOIS/RDAP info and check marketplace listings
  • eleutherios - Epistemic analysis infrastructure for querying knowledge graphs

Getting Started with OpenClaw

To get started with OpenClaw, follow these steps:

  1. Install Node.js on your system
  2. Run the onboarding wizard: openclaw onboard
  3. Configure your messaging platform connections
  4. Set up your AI provider credentials
  5. Customize your skills and integrations
